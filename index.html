Complete Reference
Commands
daily
Claim your daily blood and hex. Resets every 24 hours. Keep your streak alive to unlock the 3-day streak bonus task. VIP members get double blood automatically.
→ Regular use: just run it once a day
earn
See all available ways to earn. Shows your daily status, every task, what you've completed, and what's still on cooldown.
→ Check here first if you want more currency
task complete
Complete a specific task to earn currency. Pick from the list — some reset daily, some weekly, one is one-time only. Each one tells you its cooldown.
→ Tasks: comment, share, react, streak3, firstbuy, hex5
balance
Check all three of your wallets — blood, hex, and void — plus your last 10 transactions so you can see exactly where your currency came and went.
→ Only visible to you (ephemeral)
summon
Sacrifice 50 blood + 50 hex to summon 1 void. Shows your current balances and a confirmation prompt before anything is deducted. Irreversible once confirmed.
→ Void is the rarest currency — almost no one in the Den holds it
shop
Browse everything available. SFW items are always visible. NSFW items only appear if you have the members-only role. Each item shows its price, stock remaining, and item ID.
→ Use the ID shown to buy with /buy
buy
Purchase an item by its ID. Shows a confirmation with price and your balance before deducting anything. Auto items are fulfilled instantly. Manual items go to Asmodeus to deliver.
→ Example: /buy 3
purchases
Your full purchase history. Shows ⚙️ for auto-fulfilled items and 📋 for manual ones still awaiting delivery.
waitlist join
Reserve your spot for a sold-out item. Costs 2 blood (fully refundable). You'll get a DM the moment it restocks.
→ Example: /waitlist join 5
waitlist leave
Leave a waitlist and get your 2 blood back automatically. No questions asked.
waitlist view
See all your active waitlist positions — what you're waiting on and how many people are ahead of you.
gift
Send blood or hex to another soul in the Den. Void cannot be gifted — it must be summoned or granted. Subject to daily gift limits.
→ Example: /gift @user 10 blood
gifts view
Check your inbox for unclaimed gifts from Asmodeus. Shows everything waiting for you with the amount, currency, and any message she left.
gifts claim
Claim a specific gift by its ID. Adds the currency to your wallet immediately.
→ Example: /gifts claim 3
gifts claimall
Claim everything in your inbox at once. Tells you the total across all currencies claimed.
rank
Check your current tier, score, and progress to the next tier. Score is calculated from total blood earned and spent — the more you participate, the higher you climb.
leaderboard
The top 10 most damned souls in the Den. Visible to everyone — not ephemeral. Automatically updates Discord tier roles when run.
admin statsadmin
Full Den overview. Total souls, purchases, auto vs manual order counts, currency in circulation, top-selling items, and most-waitlisted items.
admin additemadmin
Add a new item to the market. Set name, description, price, quantity, category, emoji, tag, currency, whether it's NSFW, and whether it's auto or manual fulfillment. NSFW items always cost hex regardless of what currency you set.
→ fulfillment: auto = instant · manual = posts to requests channel
admin toggleitemadmin
Show or hide any item from the shop without deleting it. Useful for temporarily pulling something out of rotation.
admin togglefulfillmentadmin
Flip any item between auto and manual fulfillment at any time. If you're too busy to fulfill something manually, flip it to auto. Flip it back when you're ready.
→ Example: /admin togglefulfillment 7
admin restockadmin
Restock a sold-out item to a new quantity. Automatically DMs everyone on the waitlist to let them know it's back.
admin giftadmin
Drop a gift into any user's inbox — blood, hex, or void — with an optional personal message. They claim it with /gifts view. They get a DM notification automatically.
admin addbalanceadmin
Directly add any currency to any user's wallet. No inbox, no claiming — goes in immediately. Use for corrections or manual bonuses.
admin givevoidadmin
Grant 1 void to a user. Limited to once per admin per calendar month. The system tracks this automatically and will block a second grant until the month resets.
Rare — use with intention
admin voidstatusadmin
Check whether you've used your monthly void grant. Shows who you gave it to and when, or confirms it's still available.
admin dropaddadmin
Schedule a timed drop. Set the item ID, how many units to add, and how many minutes from now. A countdown posts to the drops channel and updates every minute until it fires.
→ Requires DROPS_CH to be configured in bot.py
admin droplistadmin
List all scheduled and live drops with their item, quantity, scheduled time, and current status.
admin dropcanceladmin
Cancel a scheduled drop. Updates the countdown message in the drops channel automatically to show it was cancelled.
admin giftlimitadmin
Set the global daily gift cap — the maximum amount any user can gift to others in a single day across all currencies combined.
admin setgiftlimitadmin
Override the gift limit for a specific user. Useful if someone is gifting excessively or if you want to give a trusted user a higher cap.
